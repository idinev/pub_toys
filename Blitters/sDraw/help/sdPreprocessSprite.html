sdPreprocessSprite

sdPreprocessSprite performs one of the pre-processing operations on an sSprite


void sdPreprocessSprite(sSprite* pSprite,long dwOperationID,long dwColor);

Parameters

pSprite
sSprite to be preprocessed
dwOperationID
type of operation(processing) to be performed on the sSprite
dwColor
argument to the operation

Available operations:

SDPREPR_ALPHA_FROM_COLOR
constructs alpha-channel by distance of pixels from dwColor
SDPREPR_PREMULTIPLY_ALPHA
premultiplies the alpha-channel, for 2x faster sBlt(). For each ARGB pixel, preserves the A component, while RGB are made darker. Note: This darkening of RGB can/will make this sSprite useless for calls to sBltAlpha, sBltAdd,sBltAddFade! dwColor is not used here.

Return Value

None. [ASM: All registers are preserved.]

Example:

sdPreprocessSprite(pSprite1,SDPREPR_ALPHA_FROM_COLOR,0xFF00FF);

sdPreprocessSprite(pSpriteWithAlpha2,SDPREPR_PREMULTIPLY_ALPHA,0);

Remarks

SDPREPR_ALPHA_FROM_COLOR sets the SDRAWSPRITE_HASALPHA flag of the sSprite.
SDPREPR_PREMULTIPLY_ALPHA sets the SDRAWSPRITE_PREMULALPHA flag of the sSprite.
These two flags are used in sBlt() to determine which of its 3 internal transfers/blends to perform.
The first flag is used in sBltAlpha() to determine which of its 2 internal blends to perform.
