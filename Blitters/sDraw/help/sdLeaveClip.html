sdLeaveClip

sdLeaveClip restores the clipper to the state it was in just before sdEnterClip()


void sdLeaveClip();

Return Value

None. [ASM: All registers are preserved.]

Remarks

Must match every sdEnterClip with a sdLeaveClip, otherwise a GPF (crash) is underway. sDraw manages its own stack of clippers.

See Also

sdEnterClip, sdForceClip
